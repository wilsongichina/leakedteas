Who we are

We create simple TEAS preparation pages for students who want direct access to practice questions.

Built for focused TEAS practice

Many students do not want to waste time searching through complicated menus. Teas Gurus is designed around a simple flow: choose a year, choose a set, choose a subject, and start practicing.

Our goal is to make TEAS preparation easier to follow by organizing practice into clear sections such as Reading, Math, Science, and English. Each question page is designed to feel familiar, clean, and easy to use on both desktop and mobile.

The platform is for practice and study support. It is not an official ATI product and does not claim to provide official exam questions.

How Teas Gurus works

The student journey is simple from the homepage to the practice questions.

Choose a year

Students start by choosing the 2025 or 2026 practice collection.

Open a set

Each year contains practice sets that open into a dedicated set page.

Select a subject

Inside a set, students choose Reading, Math, Science, or English.

Review answers

Students answer questions and review explanations to understand why an answer is correct.
